Our Story

Hi I'm Lindsey

As the owner of Lindsey Morgan Event Design, I have a passion for designing weddings that are unique works of art. I have always been drawn to antiques, fresh flowers, and lush fabrics. I can frequently be found strolling through flea markets, or scouring antique shops for forgotten treasures. My days often involve reupholstering tufted chairs, polishing vintage silver, or mixing and matching 18th century table linens. I believe that it’s the details that make a wedding truly one of a kind.

My design style ranges from organic and bohemian to classic and refined. I use only the highest quality blooms in my floral designs. My vintage furniture and décor inventory is expertly curated and completely unique. Regardless if I’m using my own floral designs and furniture, or am sourcing from your preferred vendors, my designs are always detailed and highly personal.

I endeavor to establish a close relationship with my clients, working with each couple to design the wedding they’ve always dreamt of. Every wedding is an expression of a couple’s love, and I strive to tell their story through my designs.
