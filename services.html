Services

Full Service Event Design


Our full service event design starts with a discussion regarding your vision for the wedding. We may share pinterest inspiration or magazine clippings during this stage. We will then design a full concept board for the event, and will collaborate with you to ensure your vision is realized. We will assist you in sourcing all aspects of the design, from flowers and place settings, to linens and furniture. Finally, my team will implement your design on the day of the wedding, making sure every detail of the décor is in place.

Vintage Furniture Rentals


Our ever-expanding inventory of vintage rentals includes chairs, tables, and upholstered seating. We also have one of a kind décor items such as mismatched china, crystal glassware, antique linens, cake stands, vases, and more. If an item you desire is not currently in our inventory, we can often find the item through our network of antique dealers.

Floral Design
